French Toast

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of white bread
  • 100ml of milk
  • 1 Egg
  • A pinch of salt
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp granulated sugar

Method

  1. Slice bread diagonally and soak in milk for 1-2 mins
  2. Place egg and salt in a bowl and beat well then dip the soaked bread slices in the egg mix
  3. Heat the butter in a frying pan until it foams, then add the bread slices. Cook the french toast until golden brown, drain on kitchen roll then serve
  4. Mix sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl and sprinkle over the french toast

Improvements

  • Top with jam
  • Top with fruit
